Product walkthrough

From first open to first contribution

Journey 1 · Onboarding

Trust starts before the first contribution.

  • BVN and face verification make sure every member is a real person with a linked bank account.
  • Clear "what happens after" messaging at each step — no one joins a circle wondering if their money is safe.
Journey 2 · Create a circle

Set the rules once. The circle enforces them.

  • Live progress tracking shows who's joined, who's contributed, and who's next in the payout order.
  • Draft-saving lets you plan a circle now and send invites later when you're ready.
Journey 3 · Join a circle

An invite that explains itself.

  • Funds are held securely in the shared Ajo wallet until the circle activates — no money moves until everyone is in.
  • Real-time roster updates so you always know who's committed and who's still pending.
Journey 4 · Activate the circle

One contribution starts the whole cycle.

  • Fund your Ajo wallet directly from your linked bank account — instant and secure.
  • PIN-confirmed payments mean no accidental contributions. You review, you confirm, it goes through.
